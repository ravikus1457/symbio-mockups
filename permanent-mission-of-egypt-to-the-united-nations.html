About Us

Permanent Mission of Egypt to the United Nations

The Permanent Mission of Egypt to the United Nations serves as Egypt's official diplomatic representation at the UN Headquarters in New York. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to advance Egypt's foreign policy objectives, foster global cooperation, and promote shared values of peace, human rights, and sustainable development. We engage across all major UN organs and committees to address pressing global challenges and advocate for a more just and equitable world.

🕒 Engaging globally, available for official inquiries during business hours.
