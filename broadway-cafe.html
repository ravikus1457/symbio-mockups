About Us

Broadway Cafe

Broadway Cafe is a beloved neighborhood spot in the heart of Manhattan, where theater lovers, artists, and locals gather over expertly crafted espresso drinks and house-made pastries. We source single-origin beans from small roasters and bake fresh daily, creating the perfect backdrop for morning meetings, afternoon catch-ups, or a quiet moment between shows. Step inside and feel the energy that made Broadway legendary—now served in every cup.
