## Why this matters (talking points)
- **Most devotees visit on a phone.** The current site is hard to read on mobile — text overlaps and wraps
  awkwardly. The redesign is mobile-first and looks great on any screen.
- **It still feels like a temple.** Traditional maroon, gold, and saffron palette; Sanskrit/Om accents;
  classical serif headings — modern, but rooted in tradition.
- **Everything important is one tap away:** Book a Puja, Donate, Call the Pandit, Get Directions.
- **Easy to keep current** — festival flyers, events, and hours can be updated without fighting a clunky editor.
