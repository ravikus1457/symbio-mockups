How we keep you compliant (by design)

The safest setup for a smoke shop is pickup-only, never ship — that alone sidesteps the PACT Act shipping laws. Age is verified in three layers:

1. Browse gate
21+ date-of-birth check to enter the site.
2. ID verification
Real ID check (AgeChecker.net / Yoti) at checkout, before an order is accepted.
3. Pickup ID check
Your staff verifies a valid 21+ ID in-store, like they already do.

We build to this standard; for your exact NC obligations a short attorney review is recommended — we implement whatever standard that sets.

🪪 What ID verification actually costs (no surprises)

The verification service (AgeChecker.net or similar) charges per successful check — roughly $0.10–$0.50 each depending on volume, plus a service minimum of about $25/mo. We pass this through at cost, zero markup, itemized on your invoice.

  • At a typical 100–200 online orders/month, budget roughly $30–$60/mo total.
  • Walk-in sales cost nothing — your staff cards at the counter like always. Only online reservations trigger a paid check.
  • Returning customers can be remembered after their first verification, cutting repeat-check costs.
  • The Entry tier has no online ordering, so it has zero verification costs.

Put simply: verification is a cost of taking online orders, a few cents per order — it only grows when your orders do.
