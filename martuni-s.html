About Us

Martuni's

Martuni's has been the Bay Area's premier destination for live piano entertainment and theatrical performances since 1987. Tucked into the vibrant Valencia Corridor, we're where locals and visitors alike gather around our iconic grand piano to sing, celebrate, and connect. Every night brings a new energy—from intimate duets to full-room singalongs that'll have you believing in magic.
