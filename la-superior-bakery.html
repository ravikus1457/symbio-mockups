About Us

La Superior Bakery

La Superior Bakery has been a cornerstone of San Antonio's community for over 55 years, filling our neighborhood with the irresistible aroma of traditional Mexican breads and pastries. Every morning, our bakers arrive before dawn to mix, shape, and bake using time-honored techniques and the finest ingredients. Whether you're grabbing breakfast or stocking up for a celebration, you'll taste the difference that tradition and care make.
