Our Exquisite Lash Services

Classic Full Set

Achieve a natural, mascara-like look with one extension applied to each of your natural lashes, adding length and a subtle curl.

Hybrid Full Set

A beautiful blend of classic and volume techniques, offering texture and a fuller look than classics, yet softer than a full volume set.

Volume Full Set

Custom-made fans of multiple lightweight extensions are applied to each natural lash, creating a dramatic, dense, and fluffy appearance.

Mega Volume Full Set

The ultimate in lash luxury, this technique uses ultra-fine extensions to create incredibly dense, dark, and glamorous lashes for a truly show-stopping effect.

Lash Fills

Keep your lashes looking flawless with our regular fill appointments, recommended every 2-3 weeks to replace shed extensions and maintain fullness.

Lash Extension Removal

Gentle and safe removal of lash extensions by our trained technicians, protecting your natural lashes and ensuring comfort.
